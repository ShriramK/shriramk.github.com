Your Country Badges
Country Badges Information
Country badges are earned when you create a challenge and someone from another country unlocks your challenge. Country Badges also have a minimum badge level associated with them. So for example, you may have to have someone with a Level 3 badge from the US unlock your challenge to earn the US badge but you only need a Level 1 unlocker from France to earn the France badge. Players will be able to collect country badges for every country in the world if they want to. Once a player registers a player from each country in a zone, they will receive the region badge!

Path Badges Information
Being able to play a level can require a badge(s) as well. Levels can require badges and badges can require other badges but badges NEVER require levels. Everything just has the option of requiring a badge.
The Badges run in sequence. You need to will need to solve 10 questions to advance.
