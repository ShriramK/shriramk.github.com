About Us

SingPath ... the most fun way to practice software languages.

SingPath provides a platform to those that want to test their programming skills in a competitive and fun environment. There is a range of programming languages that you can test your skills with including Python, JavaScript, Ruby and Java with many other languages to come. Specialize in one language or show your flexibility and skill by coding in multiple languages.

Learning to code is a skill. It takes practice, not just an understanding of the theory. Just start with the first levels on one of the paths and the game will automatically ask you the next problem that you haven't solved.

Many thanks to Nick Parlante for his excellent codingBat.com, the inspiration for this website and thank you to David MacQuigg and Athar Hameed for their excellent port to the Google App Engine. And thank you to the many helpful guides on StackOverflow.com.

What are you waiting for? Sign in today and follow your chosen Path. You can be rest assured Singpath we will keep your details safe. We will contact you but it will all be in the name of the game, FUN!
